-Ports serve to standardize the type of information exchanged between computers
-Can range from 0-65535
-"Common Ports" are 0-1024
-Some good to know ones:
    -FTP:21
    -SSH:22
    -HTTP:80
    -HTTPS:443
    -SMB:445
    -RDP:3389

-These ports can be varied from. you just have to provide a (:) along with the port number to connect when using non-standard numbers.
